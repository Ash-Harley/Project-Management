# Project Management  - Hybrid Approach 

Developed a robust Agile hybrid project management framework and reports for Netflix's new innovative platform.

• Developed and coordinated a hybrid Agile-Waterfall methodology for the project integrating requirements, planning, design, development, testing and launch phases along critical path. 

• Performed market and competitor analysis of Twitch, YouTube and Facebook identifying opportunities to leverage Netflix's existing 190 country presence and 200 million+ subscriber base. 

• Owned the project budgeting, resource allocation and expenditure tracking activities and involving designs, developments, partnerships, marketing, and operations to achieve subscriber and watch time goals.
